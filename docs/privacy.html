♞ Bet Slips

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Bet Slips is a horse racing bet tracker built for personal use at the track. This policy explains how the app handles your data — and the short version is: it doesn't collect any.

No Data Collection

Bet Slips does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Everything you enter — tracks, bettors, bets, race results, and payouts — lives exclusively on your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash reporters, or third-party tracking of any kind. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

No Network Requests

Bet Slips operates entirely offline. It makes no network requests and does not communicate with any server, cloud service, or external API.

Local Storage Only

All app data is saved using your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by the app itself
  • Is removed when you uninstall the app

Your device's OS backup settings (iCloud, Google Backup) may independently back up local app data — this is outside the app's control and governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases (Pro)

The Pro subscription is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Bet Slips does not receive, store, or have access to your payment information. Purchase validation is handled by RevenueCat, which operates under its own privacy policy. RevenueCat receives only an anonymized subscriber ID — no name, email, or payment details.

Children's Privacy

Bet Slips is intended for adults aged 18 and over in jurisdictions where horse race wagering is legal. The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change materially, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on GitHub or contact the developer directly through the App Store listing.
